Free Community Use

LensLog is a free web app for sharing photography, locations, camera settings, and learning notes.

Your Photos

Only upload photos you own or have permission to share. By publishing a photo, you allow LensLog to display it publicly with the settings and notes you submit.

Public Information

Photo titles, locations, camera settings, stories, display names, and uploaded images are public. Do not publish private addresses, personal details, or anything you do not want visitors to see.

Community Rules

Do not upload copyrighted work you do not control, hateful content, harassment, sexual content involving minors, threats, private personal data, spam, malware, or anything illegal.

Moderation

LensLog may hide or remove photos, reports, profiles, or submissions that appear unsafe, abusive, spammy, illegal, or unrelated to the photography community.

No Guarantee

LensLog is provided as-is. The service may change, experience downtime, or remove content when needed for safety, maintenance, hosting limits, or legal reasons.

Removal Requests

To request removal of your own submitted photo, contact the site owner through the place where the LensLog link was shared and include enough detail to identify the photo.
