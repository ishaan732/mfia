How To Play

  1. Create a room and share the invite link.
  2. Everyone joins with their own name on their own device.
  3. The host starts the game and chits are secretly assigned.
  4. Night actions, discussion, voting, and eliminations happen in the app.
  5. Civilians win by voting out all Mafia. Mafia wins when they control the vote.

How To Play

  1. Reveal your chit privately.
  2. Mafia secretly attacks at night.
  3. Detective checks one player at night.
  4. Doctor protects one player at night.
  5. Discuss quickly once voting starts.
  6. Alive players vote someone out.

Round Flow

  1. Night actions
  2. Host resolves night + starts vote
  3. Discuss quickly
  4. Players vote
  5. Host resolves vote
  6. Repeat until Mafia or Civilians win

Role Guide

Mafia

Choose one non-Mafia player to attack at night. Win when Mafia control the vote.

Detective

Check one player at night to learn if they are Mafia or not Mafia.

Doctor

Protect one player at night. If Mafia attack that player, nobody dies.

Civilian

Use chat, voting, and deduction to remove all Mafia.
